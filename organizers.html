Workshop registration is now open for those who have managed to purchase a ticket! Please fill in the google form.

Workshop 1: Chess with Professor Jonathan Mestel

Capacity: 16

This workshop will consist of 16 simultaneous games of chess against Professor Mestel, a Double Grand Master of chess! Good luck to all of you!

Workshop 2: The Maths behind the Rubik's Cube with Christophe Jefferies

Capacity: 20

Description: Christopher can solve any cube in less than 15 seconds! Join this workshop to learn about some of the algorithms he uses, and improve your technique.

Workshop 3: What can Game Theory do for you? By Dr Borgo

Capacity: 100+

Description: Dr Dal Borgo, from UCL, will engage in an interactive lecture on Game Theory. Learn about how Game Theory can impact your every day life!

Workshop 4: Entrepreneurial tips by Charles Dupont

Capacity: 30

Description: Charles Dupont is the technical co-founder of MarkIt Education. He focuses on entrepreneurial solutions to real world problems, so sign up here to gain first hand advice on entrepreneurship and start up strategies!

Workshop 5: Meet Poker Champion Liv Boeree

Capacity: 100+

Description: Liv Boeree will offer insight into how to approach the game of poker, as well as advice on how to react to adverse strategies from competitors. She has a degree in Physics with Astrophysics from Manchester and happens to be a poker champion! She has won over 3 mil in poker tournaments!
